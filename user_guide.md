## What Is Viral Sync?

You know when you tell a friend about a great coffee shop, and they go? Right now, nobody rewards you for that. Viral Sync changes this.

When a merchant uses Viral Sync, they create a **rewards program** that actually pays you for bringing in new customers. Share a link, your friend visits the shop, and you automatically earn a commission. It's like being a micro-influencer for every local business you love.

**The best part?** You don't need to download a crypto wallet, buy any cryptocurrency, or understand blockchain. It all happens behind the scenes.

### Step 2: Tap the Link

When you tap the link, a card pops up right in your social feed (Twitter, Discord, etc.) ‚Äî no separate app needed. This is called a **Blink** (Blockchain Link, but you don't need to care about that name).

The card shows:

Tap **"Claim Rewards"** and you'll be asked to sign in. Pick whichever is easiest:

That's it. Behind the scenes, a secure rewards account is created for you instantly. You never see a "wallet address" or "private key" ‚Äî it's handled automatically, just like how Apple Pay handles your credit card.

After signing in, you'll see your rewards balance on the Home screen. It shows:

## How to Earn

### Share Your Link
Go to the **Earn** tab and you'll see your personal referral link. Share it anywhere:
- Tap **Copy** to paste it into any message
- Tap **Twitter**, **WhatsApp**, or **Telegram** for one-tap sharing

### What Happens When Your Friend Uses It
1. Your friend taps your link
2. They sign up the same way you did (Google/Apple/Email)
3. They receive their own rewards
4. **You automatically earn a commission** ‚Äî no action needed on your part

### Second-Generation Earnings
Here's where it gets interesting. If your friend shares the link with *their* friend, and that person redeems at the shop ‚Äî **you earn a smaller commission on that too**. You're building a tree of referrals, and you earn from every branch.

### FAQ for Users

**Q: Do I need cryptocurrency?**
A: No. You never buy, hold, or manage any cryptocurrency. Your rewards are tracked securely and applied automatically.

**Q: Do I need a special wallet app?**
A: No. Everything works through a normal web browser or the Viral Sync app. Your secure account is created automatically when you sign in with Google, Apple, or email.

**Q: How is my account secured?**
A: Your account uses the same security as your Google/Apple account. Behind the scenes, we use enterprise-grade security (MPC ‚Äî Multi-Party Computation) so no single server ever has full access to your account.

**Q: Can I lose my rewards?**
A: No. Your rewards are recorded permanently and can always be recovered through your Google/Apple/Email login. Even if you lose your phone, just sign in on a new device.

**Q: Is there a fee to earn or redeem?**
A: No. Earning and redeeming are completely free for you. The merchant covers all operational costs.

**Q: What happens to my data?**
A: Your referral activity is recorded on a public ledger (Solana blockchain), but it's tied to an anonymous account ID ‚Äî not your name, email, or phone number. The merchant sees "Account 7xKX...AsU redeemed 120 points" ‚Äî not "Sarah Miller redeemed 120 points."
