## How the Money Works

### You Pay Commissions, Not Upfront Costs
- **No monthly fee** â€” you only pay when results happen
- **Commission comes from rewards you created**, not from your bank account
- Think of it like this: you create 10,000 reward points. When someone redeems 100 points, the referrer might earn 10 points as commission. The cost is baked into the reward structure.

### What "Burning" Means
When a customer redeems a reward, those points are "burned" â€” destroyed permanently. This is important because it means your reward supply decreases over time, preventing inflation. You issue new rewards when you want to fuel more growth.

## FAQ for Merchants

**Q: Do I need to buy cryptocurrency?**
A: No. You never buy, sell, or hold any cryptocurrency. All costs are handled in reward points that you create. The underlying technology uses blockchain for transparency and security, but you never interact with it directly.

**Q: Do my customers need crypto wallets?**
A: No. Customers sign in with Google, Apple, or email â€” just like any other app. A secure account is created for them automatically. They'll never see words like "wallet," "private key," or "blockchain."

**Q: What if someone tries to game the system?**
A: Viral Sync has multiple anti-fraud layers:
- **Geo-verification**: Rewards can only be redeemed at your physical location
- **Rate limiting**: Maximum referrals per person per day
- **Reputation scoring**: Automatic fraud detection flags suspicious patterns
- **Hold periods**: People must hold rewards for a minimum time before sharing (prevents rapid-fire fake referrals)

**Q: What if I want to stop the program?**
A: You can pause or close your program anytime from Settings. There's a cooldown window to allow existing rewards to be redeemed, ensuring fairness to customers who already earned rewards.

**Q: How is this different from a loyalty card program?**
A: Loyalty cards reward repeat visits from the *same person*. Viral Sync rewards bringing *new people*. The two complement each other â€” keep your loyalty cards for returning customers, use Viral Sync for growth.

**Q: Can I see it working before committing?**
A: Yes. You can run the entire dashboard on devnet (test mode) with simulated data. Nothing is real until you decide to go live.

**Q: How much does it cost to run?**
A: The protocol's transaction fees on Solana are fractions of a penny (typically $0.00025 per transaction). The gas relayer handles these costs â€” you don't pay them directly or even see them. Your only cost is the commission you choose to offer referrers.
